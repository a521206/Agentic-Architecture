Global Agentic Architecture Summit 2024 Announced

Premier conference bringing together industry leaders, researchers, and practitioners for three days of insights into the future of autonomous systems.

The inaugural Global Agentic Architecture Summit 2024 has been announced, bringing together the world's leading experts in autonomous systems, enterprise architecture, and AI innovation for a three-day conference focused on the future of intelligent, self-governing systems.

Event Details

When & Where

Dates: June 15-17, 2024

Location: San Francisco Convention Center

Format: Hybrid (In-person & Virtual)

Expected Attendance

In-person: 2,500+ attendees

Virtual: 10,000+ participants

Countries: 50+ represented

Keynote Speakers

The summit will feature presentations from industry pioneers and thought leaders:

  • Dr. Yann LeCun - Chief AI Scientist, Meta - "The Future of Autonomous AI Systems"
  • Satya Nadella - CEO, Microsoft - "Enterprise AI Transformation at Scale"
  • Dr. Fei-Fei Li - Stanford University - "Human-Centered Agentic Design"
  • Jensen Huang - CEO, NVIDIA - "Computing Infrastructure for Agentic Workloads"

Conference Tracks

The summit is organized into five specialized tracks:

Enterprise Implementation

Real-world case studies and best practices for deploying agentic systems in large organizations.

Technical Architecture

Deep dives into system design, orchestration patterns, and infrastructure requirements.

Security & Governance

Frameworks for ensuring secure, compliant, and ethical autonomous system deployment.

Research & Innovation

Latest research findings and emerging technologies in agentic AI systems.

"This summit represents a unique opportunity to shape the future of enterprise AI. We're bringing together the brightest minds to tackle the most pressing challenges in agentic system deployment."

— Maria Rodriguez, Summit Director

Special Features

  • Hands-on Workshops: Interactive sessions with leading tools and platforms
  • Innovation Showcase: Demonstrations of cutting-edge agentic technologies
  • Networking Hub: Dedicated spaces for connecting with peers and experts
  • Certification Program: Professional certification in agentic architecture principles

Registration Information

Early bird registration is now open with special pricing for the first 1,000 registrants:

  • Early Bird (until March 1): $1,299 in-person, $299 virtual
  • Regular Registration: $1,599 in-person, $399 virtual
  • Student/Academic: 50% discount with valid ID
  • Group Discounts: Available for teams of 5 or more

What's Included

  • • Access to all sessions and workshops
  • • Conference materials and resources
  • • Networking events and meals
  • • One-year access to session recordings
  • • Professional certification opportunity
