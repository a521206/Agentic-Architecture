What is Agentic AI?

Artificial Intelligence is undergoing a revolutionary transformation, with Large Language Models (LLMs) leading the charge in enterprise innovation. While traditional AI systems are limited to predefined tasks, agentic AI systems are dynamic, capable of learning from their environment and adapting their strategies in real-time.

However, integrating Agentic AI requires a strategic architectural approach. This guide provides a practical framework for operationalizing agentic design patterns, ensuring these advanced capabilities are scalable, secure, and resilient within existing enterprise systems. It's designed to equip architects and developers with the methods to build sophisticated agentic systems, driving innovation and securing a competitive edge.

Key Characteristics

  • Autonomy: Ability to operate independently and make decisions
  • Goal Orientation: Focus on achieving specific objectives
  • Adaptability: Capacity to learn and adjust to new situations
  • Interoperability: Seamless integration with existing systems

Enterprise Architecture Integration

Agentic AI systems must be carefully integrated into existing enterprise architectures, considering factors such as:

  • Security and compliance requirements
  • Scalability and performance needs
  • Integration with legacy systems
  • Data governance and privacy concerns
